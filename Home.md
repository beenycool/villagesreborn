## Features Overview

### Cultural Diversity
Discover four distinct village types, each with unique architectural styles, villager behaviors, and cultural elements:

#### Roman Villages
- Classical Mediterranean architecture with forums, villas, and bathhouses
- Community-focused activities and philosophical discussions
- Specialized crafting focused on classical arts and engineering
- Events centered around debates, markets, and games

#### Egyptian Villages
- Desert architecture featuring pyramids, temples, and oases
- Traditional ceremonies and agricultural activities
- Crafting focused on hieroglyphics, artifacts, and stonework
- Events celebrating harvests, celestial alignments, and river festivals

#### Victorian Villages
- Industrial-era aesthetics with factories, townhouses, and gardens
- Social gatherings and industrial pursuits
- Crafting that blends artisanal techniques with early industry
- Events featuring exhibitions, markets, and garden parties

#### NYC-Style Villages
- Modern urban layouts with skyscrapers, shops, and parks
- Business-oriented activities and artistic pursuits
- Contemporary crafting with urban materials and designs
- Events centered around art, food, and business

### Advanced Villager AI

Villages Reborn features an innovative AI system that brings villagers to life:

- **Personality System**: Each villager has a unique personality influenced by their culture, profession, and life experiences
- **Memory and Relationships**: Villagers remember interactions with players and other villagers, forming complex social networks
- **Dynamic Activities**: Villagers engage in culturally appropriate activities based on time of day, weather, and community needs
- **Intelligent Conversations**: Talk with villagers about a wide range of topics, with responses based on their knowledge and personality
- **Emotional Responses**: Villagers react emotionally to events, gifts, and interactions, with lasting effects on relationships

### Village Events

Cultural events bring communities together and offer unique gameplay opportunities:

- **Scheduled Events**: Regular events based on in-game calendar
- **Special Celebrations**: Rare events triggered by player achievements or world conditions
- **Interactive Participation**: Join events to earn rewards and build community reputation
- **Culture-Specific Activities**: Each village type hosts unique events reflecting their cultural heritage
- **Event Rewards**: Earn exclusive items, recipes, and reputation by participating in village events

### Cultural Crafting System

Discover and master culture-specific crafting techniques:

- **Specialized Workstations**: Each culture features unique crafting stations
- **Cultural Resources**: Gather special materials found only in specific village types
- **Relationship-Based Learning**: Build connections with villagers to learn new recipes
- **Progressive Unlocks**: Start with basic cultural items and advance to master-level crafting
- **Functional and Decorative Items**: Create both practical tools and beautiful cultural artifacts

## Configuration

Villages Reborn is highly customizable to suit your gameplay preferences:

### Setup Modes
- **Quick Start**: Jump in with pre-configured AI settings (recommended for most players)
- **Advanced Setup**: Configure custom LLM parameters for enhanced AI capabilities
- **Basic Mode**: Play with cultural villages but minimal AI features

### Performance Optimization
- Automatic system detection to adjust AI complexity
- Manual settings to fine-tune performance
- Scalable features to accommodate various hardware capabilities

### Mod Integration
- Compatible with most popular Minecraft mods
- Special integration with select village and NPC enhancement mods
- Support for custom resource packs

## Commands

Villages Reborn adds several useful commands to enhance gameplay:

- `/village info`: View details about the nearest village
- `/village events`: List active and upcoming events
- `/village culture <type>`: Get information about a specific culture
- `/villager personality <ID>`: View a villager's personality traits
- `/village reputation`: Check your standing in nearby villages
- `/village generate <culture> <radius>`: Create a custom village
- `/village reset_reputation`: Reset your reputation in the current village
