# Cultural Villages Guide

This guide covers everything you need to know about the different cultural villages in Villages Reborn.

## Overview

Cultural villages are unique communities that represent different historical and modern cultures. Each village type has its own:
- Distinctive architecture and building materials
- Unique villager behaviors and daily routines
- Special crafting recipes and workstations
- Cultural events and festivals
- Themed decorations and artifacts
- Custom structures and landmarks

## Available Cultures

### Roman Villages

#### Architecture & Structures
- **Roman Forum**: Central community space for meetings, debates, and trading
- **Bathhouses**: Social gathering spaces with decorative pools and mosaics
- **Villas**: Luxurious homes with atriums, gardens, and specialized rooms
- **Temples**: Religious structures dedicated to various deities
- **Aqueducts**: Water transportation systems connecting village sections
- **Amphitheaters**: Event spaces for performances and competitions

#### Villager Behavior
- Schedule includes bathing, forum debates, marketplace trading
- Value philosophy, politics, and cultural discussions
- Formal greetings and ceremonial interactions
- Strong emphasis on community gatherings and public life

#### Special Features
- Mosaic floor decorations with intricate patterns
- Columns and archways made of marble and stone
- Fountains and water features throughout the village
- Statues depicting cultural figures and mythological characters
- Roman numerals on signs and buildings

#### Cultural Crafting
- **Forum Workshop**: Craft civic decorations and architectural elements
- **Bath Artisan Table**: Create mosaics, sculptures, and bathing items
- **Philosopher's Study**: Make scrolls, writing implements, and knowledge items

#### Events
- **Forum Debates**: Engage in philosophical discussions for knowledge rewards
- **Market Days**: Special trading events with unique goods
- **Gladiatorial Games**: Combat events with prizes and reputation rewards
- **Feast of Saturn**: Annual celebration with special foods and decorations

### Egyptian Villages

#### Architecture & Structures
- **Pyramids**: Monumental structures housing treasure rooms and cultural artifacts
- **Temples**: Religious buildings with hypostyle halls and hieroglyphic decorations
- **Bazaars**: Open-air markets with specialized stalls
- **Oases**: Water sources surrounded by palm trees and crops
- **Sphinx Statues**: Guardian monuments marking village entrances
- **Nilometers**: Structures measuring water levels reflecting seasonal changes

#### Villager Behavior
- Schedule includes dawn ceremonies, crafting activities, and evening rituals
- Focus on tradition, agriculture, and religious observances
- Respectful greetings with cultural phrases
- Strong connection to natural cycles and celestial events

#### Special Features
- Hieroglyphic inscriptions on walls and monuments
- Palm trees and desert flora throughout the village
- Stone tablets containing cultural knowledge
- Cat statues and animal-themed decorations
- Colorful market awnings and fabrics

#### Cultural Crafting
- **Hieroglyph Table**: Create inscribed items and cultural artifacts
- **Papyrus Workshop**: Craft scrolls, maps, and knowledge items
- **Stoneworker's Bench**: Make architectural elements and sculptures

#### Events
- **Pyramid Ceremonies**: Nighttime rituals with special rewards
- **Harvest Festival**: Agricultural celebration with food-based rewards
- **Nile Festival**: Water-themed events with trading opportunities
- **Solar Alignment**: Seasonal event marking astronomical events

### Victorian Villages

#### Architecture & Structures
- **Town Square**: Central gathering place with clock tower and benches
- **Terraced Houses**: Row homes with chimneys and small gardens
- **Factories**: Industrial buildings with machinery and smokestacks
- **Public Gardens**: Ornate green spaces with gazebos and flowerbeds
- **Market Hall**: Covered market with specialized vendor stalls
- **Train Station**: Transportation hub connecting to other areas

#### Villager Behavior
- Schedule includes work hours, tea time, and evening social gatherings
- Focus on industry, commerce, and social etiquette
- Formal greetings and class-conscious interactions
- Emphasis on manners and proper social conduct

#### Special Features
- Cobblestone streets with gas lamps
- Ironwork fences and decorative elements
- Flower gardens and trimmed hedges
- Clockfaces and timepieces throughout the village
- Smokestacks and chimneys emitting particle effects

#### Cultural Crafting
- **Industrial Workshop**: Create mechanical components and tools
- **Artisan's Table**: Craft decorative items and intricate goods
- **Inventor's Bench**: Make innovative devices and mechanisms

#### Events
- **Garden Parties**: Social gatherings with exclusive trading opportunities
- **Industrial Exhibitions**: Showcase new crafting techniques and inventions
- **Holiday Markets**: Seasonal trading events with unique items
- **Queen's Day**: Annual celebration with special activities and decorations

## Cultural Mechanics

### Generation
- Villages spawn naturally in appropriate biomes based on culture type
- Each culture has preferred terrain and environmental conditions
- Generation frequency is configurable in mod settings
- Villages scale based on available terrain and generation parameters

### Progression
1. **Discovery**: Find and identify village type through architecture and landmarks
2. **Integration**: Build rapport with villagers through positive interactions
3. **Participation**: Join cultural events and village activities
4. **Learning**: Unlock crafting recipes by building relationships
5. **Mastery**: Create cultural artifacts and special items

### Village Development
- Villages can expand through player assistance
- Adding new buildings increases villager capacity
- Improving facilities unlocks additional features
- Defending villages from threats improves reputation

## Village Influence System

### Cultural Zones
- Villages project a cultural influence in surrounding area
- Different structures extend influence in different ways:
  - **Landmarks**: Create large influence zones
  - **Housing**: Increases villager capacity
  - **Cultural Buildings**: Boost event frequency
  - **Crafting Areas**: Enhance recipe availability

### Reputation Effects
- Higher reputation provides:
  - Better trading prices
  - Access to rare recipes
  - Invitation to exclusive events
  - Assistance from villagers
  - Special dialog options

## Villager Interactions

### Cultural Dialog
- Each culture has unique conversation topics and styles
- Villagers reference their cultural heritage and traditions
- Special greetings and farewell phrases
- Culture-specific jokes and stories
- Historical and traditional knowledge sharing

### Relationship Building
- Learn cultural customs through dialog
- Bring appropriate gifts based on culture
- Participate in cultural traditions
- Help with culturally relevant tasks
- Defend the village from threats

## Tips & Strategies

### Finding Villages
- Look for culture-specific landmarks visible from a distance:
  - Roman: Aqueducts and temples
  - Egyptian: Pyramids and obelisks
  - Victorian: Clock towers and smokestacks
  - NYC: Skyscrapers and parks
- Use the `/locate structure villages_reborn:village_<culture>` command
- Check appropriate biomes for each culture type
- Look for cultural paths leading to villages

### Building Reputation
- Complete village tasks offered by villagers
- Bring appropriate gifts based on cultural preferences
- Defend villages from hostile mobs
- Participate actively in cultural events
- Respect cultural customs in interactions

### Village Development
- Repair damaged structures to improve village condition
- Help expand village boundaries by building compatible structures
- Upgrade crafting stations to unlock additional recipes
- Create trade routes between different cultural villages
- Enhance village defenses against threats

## Customization

### Configuration Options
- Adjust village generation frequency per culture type
- Modify event occurrence rates
- Change villager density and behavior patterns
- Customize AI complexity and interaction depth
- Fine-tune performance settings for different hardware

### Mod Integration
- Compatible with structure and decoration mods
- Supports custom resource packs for visual enhancement

- Can be extended with custom cultures through add-ons
- Configurable compatibility settings for other mods

## Troubleshooting

If villages aren't generating properly:
1. Check world settings and mod configuration
2. Verify biome compatibility for the culture type
3. Ensure no conflicting mods are affecting generation
4. Try using the village generation command
5. Create a new world if necessary

If village events aren't occurring:
1. Check event frequency settings
2. Ensure village reputation is high enough
3. Verify village has appropriate cultural buildings
4. Check time of day and seasonal conditions
5. Try reloading the chunk containing the village

See the [Troubleshooting Guide](Troubleshooting) for more detailed help.
