# Cultural Villages Guide

This guide covers everything you need to know about the different cultural villages in Villages Reborn.

## Overview

Cultural villages are unique communities that represent different historical and modern cultures. Each village type has its own:
- Distinctive architecture
- Unique villager behaviors
- Special crafting recipes
- Cultural events and festivals
- Themed decorations
- Custom structures

## Available Cultures

### Roman Villages
- **Architecture**: Forums, bathhouses, villas, temples
- **Notable Structures**: 
  - Roman Forum (community center)
  - Public Bathhouse (social hub)
  - Temples (cultural ceremonies)
- **Villager Behavior**: Emphasis on community, trade, and cultural events
- **Special Features**:
  - Roman-style markets
  - Public gathering spaces
  - Classical decorations

### Egyptian Villages
- **Architecture**: Pyramids, temples, market squares
- **Notable Structures**:
  - Pyramids (cultural landmarks)
  - Temples (ceremonial spaces)
  - Bazaars (trading hubs)
- **Villager Behavior**: Focus on tradition, crafting, and ceremonies
- **Special Features**:
  - Desert-adapted buildings
  - Hieroglyphic decorations
  - Oasis gardens

### Victorian Villages
- **Architecture**: Townhouses, markets, gardens
- **Notable Structures**:
  - Town Square
  - Market Hall
  - Public Gardens
- **Villager Behavior**: Emphasis on etiquette and social gatherings
- **Special Features**:
  - Cobblestone streets
  - Lampposts
  - Decorative ironwork

### NYC-Style Villages
- **Architecture**: Modern buildings, shops, parks
- **Notable Structures**:
  - High-rise buildings
  - Street markets
  - Urban parks
- **Villager Behavior**: Fast-paced, business-focused
- **Special Features**:
  - Street art
  - Modern amenities
  - Urban gardens

## Cultural Mechanics

### Generation
- Villages spawn naturally in appropriate biomes
- Each culture has preferred biome types
- Generation frequency is configurable
- Villages scale based on terrain

### Progression
1. **Discovery**: Find and identify village type
2. **Integration**: Build rapport with villagers
3. **Participation**: Join cultural events
4. **Mastery**: Learn cultural crafting

### Village Influence
- Villages affect surrounding area
- Cultural spread mechanics
- Influence on local resources
- Impact on other villages

## Cultural Events

### Types of Events
- Cultural festivals
- Seasonal celebrations
- Trading fairs
- Community projects
- Special ceremonies

### Participation
1. Talk to villagers about upcoming events
2. Prepare required materials
3. Join event activities
4. Earn rewards and reputation

## Cultural Crafting

### Crafting Stations
Each culture has unique crafting stations:
- Roman: Forum Workshop
- Egyptian: Temple Crafting Table
- Victorian: Artisan's Workbench
- NYC: Modern Workshop

### Recipe Unlocks
- Build relationships with villagers
- Participate in cultural events
- Complete village quests
- Trade for recipe knowledge

### Special Items
Each culture offers unique craftable items:
- Decorative blocks
- Cultural artifacts
- Special tools
- Ceremonial items

## Villager Interactions

### Cultural Dialog
- Unique conversation topics
- Culture-specific greetings
- Historical references
- Traditional sayings

### Relationship Building
- Learn cultural customs
- Participate in traditions
- Trade cultural items
- Attend events

## Tips & Strategies

### Finding Villages
- Use `/locate village` command
- Look for cultural markers
- Check preferred biomes
- Follow trade routes

### Building Reputation
- Respect cultural norms
- Complete village tasks
- Attend events regularly
- Trade fairly

### Village Development
- Help expand village
- Protect from threats
- Contribute resources
- Support cultural growth

## Customization

### Configuration Options
- Village generation frequency
- Cultural spread rate
- Event frequency
- AI behavior settings

### Mod Integration
- Compatible with structure mods
- Supports custom textures
- Allows resource pack modifications
- Extensible for addons

If villages aren't generating properly:
1. Check world settings
2. Verify biome compatibility
3. Review mod configuration
4. Create new world if needed
